FAQ

  • How big are the duckies?
  • Each ducky is 7cm x 6.75cm x 6cm (length x height x width). All our products come in one size.

  • How much is a ducky?
  • Each of our pre-designed duckies cost $6.99, excluding shipping. Custom-made duckies start at $12.99.

  • How long does it take to ship?
  • Our location is based in Boston and shipping times to different regions/countries may vary.
    • Northeast region: 3-6 business days
    South, Midwest regions: 6-8 business days
    Frontier, Pacific regions: 8-12 business days
    International: 14-18 business days

  • What are the duckies made of?
    • The duckies are made of BPA-free silicone, which can withstand more heat and pressure and allows it to last longer compared to the traditional rubber ducks.

  • Are the duckies safe for pets? What about for children?
  • Yes, the duckies are safe for both children and pets.

  • How do I clean the duckies?
  • Simply wash with regular soap and clean water!

  • Can I return a ducky and get a refund?
  • Currently, we are not processing any returns/refunds. We apologize for the inconvenience.
